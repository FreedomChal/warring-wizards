Warring wizards is an online combat clicker game, in which each player uses their magical energy and coins, which increase over time, to improve their strength and destroy their enemies.

Each player has 6 main stats:

  • HP
  • Max HP
  • Healing Rate
  • Armor
  • Attack Strength
  • Income

HP is Health Points. If you lose all your HP, you die.

Max HP is the maximum amount of HP you can heal.

Healing Rate is the amount of HP you heal per minute.

Armor is a form of protection which reduces your HP loss when you are attacked. Damage you take is divided by Armor (eg. 2 armor will half the amount of damage you take).

Attack strength is the amount of attack damage you do for each unit of Energy used.

Income is the amount of coins you receive per minute.

Also, each player has two constantly increasing stats: Coins and Energy

Coins are the currency used to purchase upgrades. It grows at a rate of income per minute.

Energy is your magical energy, used for offensive magic. Over time, the rate at which it increases will increase, independent of stats you purchase with coins. Using your energy will make it grow faster, but using the energy also depletes it.

For 5 Coins, you can upgrade one of your stats.

Later, there will be more features, possibly including teams, specialties, some form of investing coins, and private games.
